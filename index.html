What Everything Is
The Your Views row up top is filtered to you specifically. The Team Views row is everyone, everything, unfiltered.
Your Views (tabs, above)
My Calendar
Log time, see your day on the calendar, and view every task assigned to you or anyone else on the team.
My Project Board
Only the projects you're actually involved in — created by you, or with a task assigned to you.
My Tickets
Tickets assigned to you or that you created. You can add a new one right from here.
Team Views (top row)
Team Project Board
Every project on the team, filterable by status. Open one to see and edit its tasks.
Tickets
The full open-ticket queue — claim one, or convert it into a real Task or Project.
Recurring Items
Set up something once (like "Birthday cards — El Paso"), and it spawns a real task automatically each month.
Data
Team-wide stats, plus a breakdown by employee and by category. Only visible after entering Maintenance Access.
