Recurring Items

Set It Once, It Shows Up On Schedule
How this works
Set something up once with a name, a category, and a day of the month. On its day — a day of the month, or a day of the week — it drops into the ticket queue automatically. You can file it inside a project or sub-project. When someone completes that ticket, it turns into a task with the time logged against it.

Tickets

The Queue
How this works
Drag tickets across the board as work moves.
  • To Do — not started.
  • In Progress — someone's on it.
  • Waiting — stuck until someone else comes back.
  • Done — finished. Asks for time and a project, then files it as real work.
Click a ticket for its fields, files, steps and conversation.
  • Ask a Question — offer one-click choices, and it parks the ticket in Waiting and emails them. Their answer sends it back to In Progress.
  • Templates — saved ticket recipes the whole team shares. Pick one and it builds the ticket for you, steps and all.
